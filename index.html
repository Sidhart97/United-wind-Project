UNITED WIND LOGISTICS offers one-stop-shop logistic solutions for its clients. Services include, but are not limited to; analysis of the transportation and project requirements, sourcing and securing of suitable tonnage, project coordination and management.

Through our team of in-house naval architects, engineers and project managers UNITED WIND LOGISTICS is able to offer operational services, such as:

  • HSE planning and supervision
  • Hazard Analysis and risk assessment
  • Stowage plans, lifting plans and cargo securing plans
  • Design and procurement of grillage and weight spreading
  • Vessel planning and coordination of port calls, bunkering, crew changes and port captain services

UNITED WIND LOGISTICS is able to plan and supervise the whole process of the logistical requirements and safeguard a successful project execution.
